# Multi-modal Imbalance Learning: A Survey (BalanceBenchmark)

**Authors:** (e.g., Y. Xu et al.)
**Venue:** arXiv / IEEE TPAMI
**Link:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2308.xxxxx

## Abstract
A comprehensive survey specifically covering "Multimodal Imbalance Learning". This is the direct theoretical umbrella for your research. The survey categorizes methods into:
1.  **Gradient Modulation** (OGM, blending)
2.  **Objective Functions** (Regularization, distillations)
3.  **Data Augmentation** (synthetic missingness)
It also introduces **BalanceBenchmark**, a standardized suite to test if a model is "lazy" or "greedy".

## Key Contributions
- **Taxonomy:** Clear classification of all "dominance" fighting techniques.
- **Benchmark:** A set of datasets and metrics to measure dominance.
- **Future Work:** Identifies "dynamic" imbalance (changing during training) as a key open area.
